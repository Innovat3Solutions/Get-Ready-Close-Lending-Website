Typography
Primary Typeface

Aa

Alibaba Sans

Alibaba Sans is our primary typeface. It is clean, modern, and highly legible, perfect for conveying financial clarity and professionalism. It should be used for headlines and major brand statements.

Body Text
This is an example of body text using MiSans Regular. It is used for all long-form content, letters, and detailed descriptions. The font size should generally be between 16px and 18px for optimal readability.
